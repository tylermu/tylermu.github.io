About Me
Hello! I'm Tyler, and I am currently a computer science student at the University of St. Thomas. While my academic achievements have been commendable, they only scratch the surface of who I am. Prior to my time at St. Thomas, I delved into graphic design at a community college, which paved the way for an invaluable unpaid internship. The same company recognized my potential, bringing me on part-time after my internship, and later helped me to secure a graphic design role for the University of St. Thomas. Remarkably, most of my graphic design skills were self-taught, like my proficiency in video editing, photography, guitar, music production, custom e-commerce website creation, and website design. I mention all these things because they include my hobbies and passions, in addition to coding, and they help make me a well-rounded person. Since arriving at St. Thomas I have learned countless coding languages, mastered coding concepts, and written numerous programs. I'm driven by a genuine passion for learning; it's a trait that defines me. My ability to adapt and master new skills quickly presents an undeniable advantage that I am eager to contribute to your team. I invite you to explore my resume and projects section to gain deeper insights into my professional journey, encompassing both work experiences and coding-related projects.
